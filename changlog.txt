            To get a Dropbox App Key, you need to create a Dropbox App in the Dropbox Developer Console. Here are the steps:
            1. Go to the [Dropbox Developer Console](https://www.dropbox.com/developers/apps).
            2. Sign in with your Dropbox account or create a new account if you don't have one.
            3. Click on "Create App".
            4. Choose "Scoped Access".
            5. Choose "Full dropbox" or "App folder" depending on your needs.
            6. Enter a name for your app and click "Create App".
            7. Once your app is created, you will be taken to the app's settings page. Here you will find your "App key" and "App secret".
            8. Copy the "App key" and use it in your `.env` file as the value for `DROPBOX_APP_KEY`.
